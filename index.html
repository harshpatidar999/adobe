XD Cloud Content Integrations

Start integrating todayStart integrating today.

With the XD Cloud Content APIs, you can bring design work created in XD directly to your product or service.

Bringing designers and developers together

The Adobe XD and Jira Software Cloud integration uses the XD Cloud
Content APIs to let designers associate XD prototypes and design
specs with Jira issues. This keeps projects organized while providing
developers with everything they need to know in the environment they
know.
